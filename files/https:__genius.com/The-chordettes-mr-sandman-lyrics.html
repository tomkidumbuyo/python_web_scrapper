About “Mr. Sandman”

“Mr. Sandman” is euphemism for coming-of-age of a young woman in terms of her sexuality and dark fantasies. Despite having a magical, mythical role, the Sandman is a metaphor for sexual desire.

The song was inducted into the Grammy Hall of Fame in 2002 and was named as one of the Songs of the Century by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) and National Endowment for the Arts (NEA).

  • What is a "rover"?

    “A vagabond, a tramp, an unsteady, restless person, one who by habit doesn’t settle down or marry”.
